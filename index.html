WHO I AM

WHO I AM

My name is Aleksandra and if you have found this link though
my instagram (@tumbleflipsplit) you probably already know
a bit about me.

My original fitness story is long and with many ups and
downs which I am sure many people can relate to and if not I
am happy for you.

I started gymnastics at 18 years old and the only training
option I had was with 10 year olds. Since I had no other
options I took it! It was hard, people didn't understand why I
wanted to do it. I was "TOO OLD" for gymnastics.

I had to push though a lot of tough time during training and it
was hard to find coaches that were good at coaching adults.
This is when I decided to study fitness and become a coach
myself.

I have now been coaching gymnastics, fitness and flexibility
for 4 years and hope to help anyone that wants to become
the most flexible, fit, healthy and most importantly HAPPY
version of themselves. I believe that fitness and flexibility
should be made simple and accessible to people anywhere
and at any age!!!!
